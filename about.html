Welcome to Athanotech

Athanotech Solutions Ltd. is a global hub of interdisciplinary innovation where creative visionaries, strategic consultants, and technical experts converge to solve intricate business problems.

At AthanoTech, we are passionate about providing business solutions for a sustainable future for our stakeholders. We fuse aesthetics, intuitive insight, engineering expertise and cutting-edge technology to tackle complex business challenges and craft a more sustainable future.

Since founded in 2024 by Ikenna Athan Oparaji, we have grown form a small local company to a trusted international solution provider with our international business partners.

Mission Statement

"Empowering businesses and communities to thrive through innovative, sustainable solutions."

Vision Statement

"Catalyzing a sustainable future where technology and human values harmonize."

About The CEO

Ikenna Athan Oparaji

Managing Director, Athanotech Solutions Ltd.

Ikenna Athan Oparaji is a visionary leader, accomplished engineer, and creative writer. With a strong academic background, including a Merit in MSc Management with Business Analytics from Bournemouth University, United Kingdom, he brings a unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen to Athanotech.

A prolific engineer, Ikenna has received training and certifications from renowned institutions, including Testlink Services Ltd (United Kingdom), Solartech International (United Arab Emirates), Jotun (Middle East, India, and Africa), Mascoat Insulation (USA), and IMG Composites Ltd (Aberdeen, United Kingdom), in Asset Integrity Management.

Ikenna's thought leadership has been featured in various engineering journals, showcasing his expertise and passion for innovation. Beyond his technical accomplishments, Ikenna is a gifted poet and writer, using his creative voice to inspire and teach valuable life lessons. As a dedicated mentor, he has guided numerous students who have gone on to become successful engineers, doctors, lawyers, and more. Ikenna's philosophy is that engineering offers a unique perspective on the world, influencing his poetry and approach to leadership. He seeks to connect with like-minded global citizens who share his vision for a sustainable future.
